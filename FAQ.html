Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you deliver to?
We offer delivery to various locations. Please enter your zip code at checkout to confirm availability in your area.
How can I place an order?
You can browse our selection of flowers and arrangements, add your desired items to the cart, and follow the checkout process to place your order online.
What is the delivery time?
We provide same-day delivery for orders placed before a certain time. For specific delivery options, please check our delivery policy during checkout.
Can I schedule a delivery for a future date?
Yes, you can select a specific delivery date at checkout for your order.
What if I want to change my order after it's been placed?
Please contact our customer service as soon as possible. We can accommodate changes if your order hasn't already been processed.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards and online payment platforms. Check our payment page for a complete list.
Do you offer same-day delivery?
Yes, we offer same-day delivery on eligible items. Ensure your order is placed before the specified cut-off time.
What if the flowers I ordered are out of stock?
If an item is out of stock, we will contact you to offer a suitable substitute or refund your order.
What is your return policy?
If you are not satisfied with your order, please contact us within 24 hours. We aim to resolve any issues with replacements or refunds.
How do I care for my flowers after delivery?
We provide care instructions with every order to help you keep your flowers fresh and beautiful for as long as possible.
